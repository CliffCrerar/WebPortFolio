Profession

At the moment I spend my working hours at a company named SurTech (pty) ltd as a solutions architect. The work revolves around product development in data analytics for the finance, tax and audit sector. I am part of a high preforming agile team. My role is to devise solutions for problems identified by the product manager. In my daily tasks I am expected to work with the team to achieve our bi-weekly goals, conduct research on topics that require decision making, familiar myself with enterprise resource system data structures, such as Oracle E-Business suite and Microsoft dynamics. I must do this in order to design standard data structures apply predefined audit analytics to data extracted from theses ERP systems. Our analytics suite is named ACL Essentials because the analytics are designed to run in ACL. ACL is a special data analysis application, a leader in its class, that allows user to handle, interpret and analyse huge amounts of raw data. I am also expected to develop applications for tax, as well as applications to assist the implementation of audit analytics. My job is highly satisfying.
